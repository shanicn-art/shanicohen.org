Shani Cohen

Welcome! I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor equivalent) at the Hebrew University economics department, studying economics theory and behavioral economics. I obtained my PhD from Harvard University in 2025.

You can download my CV here.

I hold two master's degrees in mathematics, from the Hebrew University of Jerusalem (MSc) and from the University of Cambridge (MASt). I am also a fellow of Molad, the center for the renewal of Israeli democracy.

Feel free to reach out by email s.cohen@mail.huji.ac.il or on twitter @shani_cn.
